Creative Coding Showcase — Summer School 2021

This site showcases the outcomes of the Creative Coding workshop conducted by Johannes Jakob and Felix Koppe at Dessau Department of Design’s summer school in 2021. Two students, Daniela Heinz and Jasmin Schmidt, participated in the workshop, and due to the small group size, the working atmosphere was very relaxed and focused.

The workshop was designed for people without any programming knowledge and covered the fundamentals of creative coding with p5.js and also a few bits of p5.play for gameplay mechanics.

Browse through the small experiments and games Daniela and Jasmin have created during the five-day workshop.
